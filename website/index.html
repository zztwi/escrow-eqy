ticket lifecycle

Every trade follows the same visible path.

Buyer, seller, staff, deposit proof, delivery proof, disputes, and payout confirmations stay attached to one ticket ID.

01

Ticket opened

Staff or users start an escrow with buyer, seller, amount, fee, and trade description.

pending
02

Deposit submitted

The buyer submits PayPal proof. eqy forwards it to the proof channel with ticket details.

deposit submitted
03

Funds confirmed

Staff checks the PayPal vault and confirms the deposit before delivery continues.

funded
04

Delivery and proof

The seller delivers inside the ticket. Users can attach payment, chat, identity, or delivery proof.

in progress
05

Release requested

The buyer releases the trade when delivery is complete, or opens a dispute if something is wrong.

release
06

Payout confirmed

Staff confirms the PayPal payout ID, closes the ticket, and sends transcripts to both sides.

Private escrow tickets
